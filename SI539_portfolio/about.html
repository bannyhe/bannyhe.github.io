About Me

I am Mu He, a first-year master student specializing in Human-Computer Interaction at the University of Michigan School of Information.

As a creative, enthusiastic and rigorous designer, I regard user-center design, visual beauty, and creativity as my design philosophy, and explore the potential needs of consumers through research and simulation, via endless pursuit of perfection!
